Welcome

Welcome to Association of Radiation Oncologists of India (AROI) TN PY Tirunelveli Chapter Conference 2025. Join us on August 9th & 10th 2025, at the scenic town of courtallam, as we delve into the theme "Redefining Boundaries in Radiation Oncology". This conference brings together leading experts, practitioners, and researchers to explore the latest advancements and innovations in radiation oncology.

About the Conference

Redefining Boundaries in Radiation Oncology

The AROI TN PY Chapter proudly presents its annual conference, a unique opportunity to exchange knowledge, foster collaborations, and inspire new ideas. This two-day event will feature keynote sessions, panel discussions, and workshops led by renowned specialists in the field.
